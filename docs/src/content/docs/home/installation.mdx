## Technical Requirements

To ensure a stable audio experience, verify that your environment meets the following baseline requirements:

<Steps>
1. **Lavalink Server**: Version **4.0.0** or higher is required for the full feature set.
2. **TypeScript**: Version **5.4+** is recommended for standard-compliant module augmentation.
3. **Hardware**: At least **512MB RAM** allocated for your bot process (recommended for large queues).
</Steps>
